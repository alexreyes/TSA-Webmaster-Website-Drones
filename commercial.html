Commercial Uses of Augmented Reality

By: TSA Students

1. DIY Car Repair:

One way in which augmented reality can be useful in a commerical aspect is with DIY car repair. In an idea world, you woudl not have to be an expert mechanic to fix some problems you're having with your car. As augmented reality advances, it seems more and more likely that someday we'll be able to fix our engine by simply holding up our phone to it and following the on-screen instructions.

2. Learning to cook:

At some point in everyone's lives, there comes a time when you need to learn how to cook. Although cooking can be hard for beginners and veterans alike, it doesn't have to be in a world where Augmented Reality exists. For example, you can learn to cook something like an omlete by holding up your phone to your kitchen pan, and following on-screen instructions. Although only prototypes exist at the moment for this, it's not too far off!
