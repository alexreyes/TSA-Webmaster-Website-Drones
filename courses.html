Recreational Uses of Augmented Reality

By: TSA Students

1. Snapchat Filters:

Possibly the most popular use of recreational Augmented reality are snapchat filters. A essential counterpart of a popular social network for teens, snapchat filters offer an easy way to make yourself look funny.

2. Videogames:

Videogames will most likely someday be the most popular use of augmented reality technology. The use of the technology in games allows for a more realistic way to play. Already, tech giants like Microsoft are betting on it being the next big thing. Currently, Microsoft is working on the Hololens, a Augmented reality headset for both recreational and proffessional use.
