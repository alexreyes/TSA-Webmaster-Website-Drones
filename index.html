Recreational uses for Augmented Reality

Safety

There are several safety concerns that need to be taken into account with augmented reality. With the rise of popular AR games like Pokemon Go, many people have not been careful with being aware of their surroundings. Making this mistake with augmented reality could result in getting injured such as getting hit by a car, or falling, etc.

Available Tech

One of the technologies for augmented reality is your own cell phone. With the rise of apps like Snapchat and Pokemon GO, it's easier than ever to try it out for yourself. Additionally, Microsoft is creating a more advanced unit called the HoloLens which promises to offer more realistic Augmented Reality powered by a PC.

Future Advances

Augmented reality still has a long way to go in the future. There is still much room for improvement in terms of things like ease of access and realism. Right now much of the available Augmented Reality programs are basically being used for demoes. Hopefully in the future we'll be able to move towards more practical uses.
